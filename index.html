Meet Dr. Sarah Mitchell

With over 15 years of experience in clinical psychology, I specialize in helping individuals and couples navigate life's most challenging moments. My approach combines cognitive behavioral therapy with mindfulness techniques to create lasting positive change.

I hold a Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology from Stanford University and am a licensed therapist in California. My practice is rooted in empathy, trust, and a commitment to your mental wellbeing.

Whether you're dealing with anxiety, depression, relationship issues, or simply seeking personal growth, I'm here to support your journey toward a healthier, more fulfilling life.
