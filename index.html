Meet Dr. Sarah Mitchell

As a clinical psychologist, I help individuals, couples, and families strengthen their mental health and well-being.

With over 15 years of experience, I work with my clients to improve their quality of life, strengthen relationships, and support personal growth. I hold a Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology from Stanford University.

I utilize evidence-based methods including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), EMDR, and couples and family counseling. I believe each person is unique and develop personalized approaches tailored to your specific needs.

The number of sessions varies depending on your individual needs and goals. Some clients see improvement in 6-12 sessions, while others benefit from longer-term therapy. We'll regularly review your progress together.
